Bangladesh's rental and property market has long been plagued by fake listings, absentee landlords, and exploitative middlemen. First-time renters, families moving cities, and Bangladeshis returning from abroad all suffered the same experience — wasted time, lost money, and broken promises.

NestIQ started in 2022 with a single conviction: every person deserves to find a home with dignity. We built technology that does the searching, an operations team that does the verification, and a concierge layer that does the hand-holding — so you can focus on what matters.

Today we operate across Dhaka with plans to expand to Chittagong, Sylhet, and Rajshahi in 2026.

Why NestIQ

Not Just Another
Property Portal

🤖
AI-Powered, Not Ad-Powered
Other portals rank listings based on who pays for placement. We rank by match quality. Your results are curated for you, not for advertisers.
🔬
Verification is Our Standard
We physically inspect properties and legally verify titles before they go live. No other platform in Bangladesh does this as a default.
🧑‍💼
A Real Human in Your Corner
Every client gets a dedicated concierge — a real person who knows the market, handles paperwork, and fights for your interests.
💰
No Hidden Fees
Our fee structure is flat and transparent. Renters pay nothing. Sellers and landlords pay a success fee only after a deal closes.
🏦
Escrow-Protected Transactions
All advance and booking payments are held in regulated escrow accounts — protecting both parties until conditions are met.
📊
Market Intelligence
We analyse thousands of transactions to give you real price benchmarks — so you never overpay or undersell in any locality.
